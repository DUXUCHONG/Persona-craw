# Persona-craw — Product

> The agent gets better every day.

---

## What It Is

An AI assistant that learns from your daily conversations. Corrections, preferences, approvals — all become training signals. No setup. No labeling. You just use it.

---

## Capabilities

### Skill Distillation

Corrections you make once become permanent behavior. The system detects recurring patterns across conversations, distills them into structured skills, and applies them automatically in future tasks.

- Say it once, it remembers forever
- Skills evolve when you change your mind
- Individual preferences compose into compound profiles (code style + testing + docs = your engineering standard)
- Only real patterns stick — one-off remarks are filtered out

### RL from Daily Words

Your everyday language is the training signal. "Perfect" is a reward. "No, not like that" is a penalty. Silence means acceptance. The AI's underlying behavior changes based on what you approve, reject, and correct — continuously, in the background.

- No training buttons or feedback forms
- Handles ambiguity and delayed feedback
- Real policy improvement, not just preference storage

### Master-Apprentice

A powerful model handles hard problems. A fast, cheap model handles daily tasks. Knowledge flows from master to apprentice — so the daily model gets more capable over time.

- Complex tasks get full power automatically
- Solutions are distilled and reused by the daily model
- The boundary shifts: tasks that needed the master last month become routine

### Local-Cloud Hybrid

A personalized model runs on your device for daily tasks. Complex tasks reach the cloud. Everything the cloud learns feeds back into your local model.

- Most interactions: instant, private, free
- Complex tasks: cloud power, seamless handoff
- Your local model grows more capable every week
